About Us

Based in Sydney, Australia

Overview

We are a social ultimate frisbee club of friends. In 2020 we formed our first social team, Wushu Warriors, and went on to eventually become Yellow Fellows. Most of us know each other through school, uni, or the wider Northern Suburbs and Hills Ultimate community. Our team culture puts friendship first and spending social time together - whether it be through dinner after games or roadtrips scattered throughout the year.
