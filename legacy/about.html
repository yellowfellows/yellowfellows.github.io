The Story So Far

How three separate teams ended up sharing one color, one vibe, and about half a roster.

Why Yellow?

Nobody fully agrees on the origin story anymore, which is honestly on brand. What started as one team's jersey order somehow spread — new teams kept forming, players kept bouncing between them, and yellow just stuck. These days "the Yellow Squad" isn't one team, it's the whole ecosystem: three rosters, a shared pool of players, and a color that's impossible to miss on the sideline.
